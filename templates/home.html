What We Offer

OPTICALS

We offer a wide range of stylish and functional prescription glasses. Our experienced staff will help you choose the perfect frames and lenses to match your lifestyle and vision needs, ensuring comfort and clarity.

CATARACT

We offer surgical correction of cataracts through clear corneal phacoemulsification, including micro-incision cataract surgery (MICS) and intraocular lens implantation. Our options for intraocular lenses include foldable, UV-protective, multifocal, and toric lenses.

GLAUCOMA

We offer comprehensive glaucoma management, including advanced diagnostics, medical therapy, laser treatments, and surgical interventions such as trabeculectomy and drainage implant surgery. Our personalized care and state-of-the-art techniques aim to preserve your vision and maintain your quality of life.

CORNEA

We provide comprehensive corneal care, including advanced diagnostics, medical treatments, and surgical procedures. Our services include corneal transplants, treatment for keratoconus, and management of corneal infections and dystrophies. We are committed to preserving your vision with personalized care and the latest medical techniques.

ORBIT OCULOPASTY SURGERY

We offer comprehensive care for conditions affecting the eyelids, orbit, tear drainage system, and surrounding structures, using the latest techniques and advanced technology to ensure the best possible outcomes for our patients

Medical Retina

We offer comprehensive care for a wide range of retinal conditions like Diabetic Retinopathy,Macular Edema & AMD, utilizing the latest diagnostic techniques and advanced treatment technologies to ensure the best possible outcomes for our patients.
