Mission Statement

To provide accessible and compassionate eye care services to underserved tribal communities in South Odisha, ensuring vision health equity through innovative solutions and community empowerment.

Vision Statement

To be a beacon of hope and excellence in eye health for tribal populations in South Odisha, pioneering sustainable healthcare models that transform lives and inspire positive change.

Core Values

  • Compassion: We treat every patient with empathy and dignity, understanding their unique needs.
  • Excellence: We strive for clinical and operational excellence to deliver the highest standards of eye care.
  • Accessibility: We ensure that all tribal communities have equitable access to our services regardless of their socioeconomic status.
  • Collaboration: We foster partnerships with local communities, NGOs, and government agencies to maximize our impact.
  • Innovation: We embrace innovation to continuously improve our services and reach more people effectively.

Goal Statement

To eradicate preventable blindness and visual impairment among tribal communities in South Odisha by 2030, through comprehensive eye care services, education, and community outreach programs.
