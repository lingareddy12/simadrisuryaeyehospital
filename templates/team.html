Meet our Expert Team of Doctors

Dr. Sukesh Gullipalli

Ophthalmologist

Dr. Sukesh Gulipalli completed his MBBS from NTR University of Health Sciences in 2012, followed by an internship at King George Hospital, Visakhapatnam. He pursued a post graduate diploma in Ophthalmology from HV Desai Eye Hospital, Pune and subsequently completed a fellowship in Medical Glaucoma. Currently, Dr. Sukesh serves as a Consultant Ophthalmologist and Glaucoma Specialist at Simadri Surya eye Hospital, Nabarangpur, Odisha, where he is dedicated to providing advanced eye care and specialized glaucoma treatment. He has performed more than 500 surgeries for treating cataract and other retinal disorders.
